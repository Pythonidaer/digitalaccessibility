Quizzes

Short interactive quizzes check recall of ideas already covered elsewhere on the site. Each question uses standard radio buttons: pick one answer, then use Check answer to see whether you are right and read a brief explanation. Quizzes can be repeated; use Reset quiz on a quiz page to clear your choices.

For open-ended study prompts (not scored), see Study questions.

Available quizzes

  • Quiz: WCAG Basics — four principles, success criteria, AA notes from the WCAG overview.
  • Quiz: ARIA Basics — what ARIA is for, roles, states, and properties from the ARIA basics page.
